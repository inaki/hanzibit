今天 jīn tiān today 我 早上 zǎo shang early morning 七点 起床 qǐ chuáng get out of bed 。 我很 累 lèi tired ， 但是我也很 高兴 gāo xìng happy / glad 。

sticky_note_2 Self-Notes & Annotations

Grammar Tip

The particle "也" (yě) always comes after the subject and before the adjective/verb.

Mnemonic

"累" (lèi) looks like a person working in a field (田) with a burden above. No wonder they are tired!
